\Large \textbf{Activity 10.4: Verification Against Analytic Solutions} \\[0.5em]
\normalsize Geophysics — Numerical Heat Flow
\end{center}

\vspace{1em}

\section*{Purpose}
To demonstrate the importance of verifying numerical models against solutions
with known behavior.

\section*{Concept}
Analytic solutions provide benchmarks for testing numerical implementations.

\section*{Tasks}
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Identify an analytic solution discussed earlier in the course that can be
  reproduced numerically.

  \item Describe what aspects of the solution should be compared (e.g., temperature
  profile shape, temporal evolution, heat flow).

  \item Explain how grid spacing and time step affect numerical accuracy.

  \item Discuss why a numerically stable solution is not necessarily accurate.
\end{enumerate}
