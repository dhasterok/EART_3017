\item {\bf [5 pts.] Thermal refraction} \\
We are exploring for geothermal resources and are trying to decide on the best
location to drill.  Based on a gravity model of the area, we have developed a
model for the structure and thermal properties of the region
(Figure~\ref{fig:refract}).
\begin{figure}[hb]
  \centerline{
    \includegraphics[]{figures/basin_geometry.eps}
  }
  \caption{Crustal model for thermal refraction.}
  \label{fig:refract}
\end{figure}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Develop a symbolic relationship for the heat flow for prospect
    location 1.

    \item Develop a symbolic relationship for the heat flow for prospect
    location 2.

    \item Based on our model is the heat flow higher at prospect 1 or 2?

    \item Draw the approximate crustal isotherms for 50\degC\ intervals
    between prospects 1 and 2. (Hint: compute depths to each isotherm
    beneath each respective borehole first.)

    \item Are basins or exposed crystalline regions better localities to search
    for geothermal resources?  Justify your answer in terms of your results.
\end{enumerate}
