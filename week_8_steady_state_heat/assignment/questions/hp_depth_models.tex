\item {\bf [5 pts.] Heat production versus depth} \\
There are three simplified models for continental heat production shown in
Figure~\ref{fig:hpmod}.  Show that the radiogenic contribution is the same for
each model.  The radiogenic contribution is defined as
\begin{equation}
    q_{\mathrm{rad}} = \int A(z) \dd{z} .
\end{equation}
Note: you will have to develop an appropriate $A(z)$ for the constant and linear
models and define the limits of integration for each model before integrating.

  \centerline{
    \includegraphics[]{pracfigs/hp_models.eps}
  }
  \caption{Mineralogical compositions of igneous rocks.}
  \label{fig:hpmod}
\end{figure}
